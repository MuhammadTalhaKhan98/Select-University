Iqra University (IU)




Iqra University is a private university primarily located in the Defence residential area of Karachi, Sindh, Pakistan. It has additional campuses in other parts of the city as well as in Islamabad. Iqra University was ranked as the top business school in Pakistan by the Higher Education Commission of Pakistan in the rankings announced in 2016.

Founded as Asian Management Institute (AMI) in 1998 by a group of Karachi-based industrialists, the university was renamed as "Iqra University" after granted university status in 2002 by HEC. The university offers undergraduate, post-graduate, and doctoral studies programmes in engineering, film studies, management sciences, administration, and related social and natural sciences disciplines. In 2015, Iqra University inaugurated its North Campus (named Iqra University North Campus).

It is one of the notable private institution of higher learning and ranked among the top-ten institution for "business" by the HEC in 2012. In addition, the university is the largest employer of doctorate holders as faculty. Also, the university has the largest research publication output of other higher learning institutions of the country.

Eligibility Criteria:


  • Intermediate/A-level or an equivalent examination with at least 60% marks; and

  • Must have studied Mathematics at HSSC level; and.

  • Admission will be based on Academic Eligibility Score, Entry Test Score and Interview..


Admission Procedure:


Step 1 : Applications are accepted through IBA Online Admissions System. The link to IBA Online Admissions System is available on IBA's website (www.iba.edu.pk).

Step 2 : To apply online, applicants need to fill out an online Admission Application Form,

Step 3 : Print a bank challan and take it to the designated bank branches to deposit the admission processing fee.

Step 4 : The bank challan duly stamped by the bank officer is then to be taken to the Admissions Office for collection of the Admit Card on the prescribed dates as per the procedure.

Step 5 : The Admit Cards issued to the applicants indicate the test center, date, and reporting time.
