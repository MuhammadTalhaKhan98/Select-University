COMSATS Institute of Information Technology (CIIT)




COMSATS, also known as COMSATS Institution of Information Technology and CIIT, is a public research university in Pakistan. It is a multi-campus institute with its headquarters located in the urban area of Islamabad. Established by the Commission on Science and Technology for Sustainable Development in the South which was established in Islamabad on 4 & 5 October 1994, the commission as of 2015 has 23 member state. The CIIT was established in 1998 with charter in August 2000. Dr. S. M. Junaid Zaidi is Founder Rector of the COMSATS Institute of Information Technology (CIIT), Pakistan’s first exclusive institute of information technology.

CIIT was ranked among top 250 Asian Universities by QS University Rankings in 2014 and nationally it is ranked top in Computer Sciences & IT category[citation needed] and 1st overall in the country according to NatureIndex. It has three stars in QS Rankings. It is also a member of Association of Commonwealth Universities It main campus located in Chak Shahzad, Islamabad has over 5,000 students and 30,000 more students in its 6 satellite campuses. Its student body also includes international students from 9 Countries. CIIT has 18 Departments which combined offer 96 degree programs and has since inception produced 850 PhDs.

Eligibility Criteria:


  • At least 60% marks in SSC (Matric) or an equivalent examination AND

  • At least 50% marks in the HSSC or an equivalent examination.

  • Must have studied Mathematics at the HSSC level.


Admission Procedure:


Step 1 : Your registration process starts from signing up yourself with valid information. After that you can login with your email or Login name to fill your appilcation form.

Step 2 : In this screen you have to fill-in your personal details. You'll have to enter your name and father / guardian name as per your Matric / O-Level certificate. Then enter your CNIC, domicile, and mobile number.

Step 3 : In this screen you'll have to fill-in your address details. Enter your permanent address and current mailing address. You'll have to enter father / guardian address if its different from permanent address. Click the checkbox 'same as permanent address' if addresses are same.

Step 4 : Fill in your educational details in this step. Starting from Matric, you'll have to enter all education degrees required for the program you are applying.

Step 5 : if you've a valid NTS result available, enter its details in this screen. If you've registered to an NTS test center and have a roll-no then mention in this screen. If you have not given NTS test then you can skip this section.

Step 6 : You'll have to select the program you want to apply in this screen. You've the option to apply in upto 3 different program choices.

Step 7 : Other miscellaneous details will be asked in this section. You can skip this section.

Step 8 : Please carefully read the checklist of documents that need to be attached with admission form.Then submit the application and take its print-out.

Step 9 : Application form completely submitted.Now you can download it or take its print-out.

Step 10 : Facilty to contact the faculty in case of any confusion/ problem is also provided. You can do that using the dialog box in the below right corner of page.
