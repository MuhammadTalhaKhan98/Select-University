National University of Computer and Emerging Sciences (FAST-NU)




The National University of Computer and Emerging Sciences is a private research university in Pakistan. It has 5 campuses based in Pakistan cities and was the first multi-campus university in Pakistan. It is consistently ranked among one of the leading and top institutions of higher learning in the country, securing its top rank in the computer sciences/IT category from the HEC in 2010.

Its Engineering programs have been accredited with Pakistan Engineering Council.Founded as a Federally Chartered University in July 2000, the National University of Computer and Emerging Sciences is a premiere University of Pakistan, renowned for quality and impact of its students in the development of local software and other industries.

The university has five modern campuses at Karachi, Lahore, Islamabad, Peshawar and Chiniot-Faisalabad.

These campuses provide world class educational environment and recreational facilities to about 9,000 students, around one quarter are female.

Eligibility Criteria:


  • Intermdiate or equivalent(Mathematics) with 50% marks, From accredited institude

  • valid NTS score as per CIIT poilcy.


Admission Procedure:


Step 1 : Admission Application Form must be filled online.

Step 2 : Applicant will deposit the Application Fee in the designated Bank.

Step 3 : Upon payment of the Application Processing Fee (and additional Admission Test Fee), the applicant will print his/her ADMIT CARD bearing the applicant’s TEST ROLL NUMBER.

Step 4 : The applicant should then visit the test campus on the specified date and time as mentioned on the Admit card.

step 5 : Admission of a student automatically stands cancelled if he or she fails in the required exam whose result was not available at the time of admission.
