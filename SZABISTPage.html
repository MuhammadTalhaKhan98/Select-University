Shaheed Zulfikar Ali Bhutto Institute of Science and Technology (SZABIST)




Shaheed Zulfikar Ali Bhutto Institute of Science and Technology (SZABIST) is a private chartered institute with multiple campuses in the residential and commercial areas of Pakistan and UAE. Its main campus is in a residential town of Karachi, Sindh, Pakistan

Begun in 1995 under the vision of its founder, Pakistani Prime Minister Benazir Bhutto, and led by Dr. Javaid Laghari as its first president and project director, SZABIST is honored and named in remembrance of Shaheed Zulfiqar Ali Bhutto. Financial endowment and research funding are coordinated by the leadership of the Pakistan Peoples Party. The university offers undergraduate, post-graduate and doctoral studies. It is a member of the Association of Commonwealth Universities of the United Kingdom and the Association to Advance Collegiate Schools of Business, as well as other international education associations. SZABIST is regarded as one of the more notable private research institutions of higher learning in Pakistan and ranked among the nation's top-ten institutions by the HEC, as of 2013. In addition, its business management programme is listed as top ranking by the publication Business Week and is ranked among other noticeable science institutions by CNN &Time and Asiaweek.

Eligibility Criteria:


  • For admission in the BBA / BS / BABS programs , the candidate must have completed O-Levels (minimum 8 passes) and A-Levels (minimum 3 passes) / 12th Grade / Intermediate with minimum 50% marks (including first year no supplementary) or equivalent from a recognized institution.

  • Candidates of O/A Level or any other foreign equivalent qualification can apply on the basis of O Level (SSC) equivalence certificate obtained from Inter Board Committee of Chairmen (IBCC) office but confirmation of their admission is subject to provision of A Level (HSSC) equivalence certificate duly obtained from IBCC, Pakistan.


Admission Procedure:


Step 1 : Submit the Application.

Step 2 : Applicant will deposit the Application Fee in the designated Bank.

Step 3 : Upon payment of the Application Processing Fee (and additional Admission Test Fee), the applicant will print his/her ADMIT CARD bearing the applicant’s TEST ROLL NUMBER.

Step 4 : The applicant should then visit the test campus on the specified date and time as mentioned on the Admit card.
